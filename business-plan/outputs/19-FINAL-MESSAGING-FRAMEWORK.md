## EVOLUTION OF THE MESSAGING

### What Changed and Why

1. **From "Workflow Intelligence" to "Operations Intelligence"**
   - More concrete: Intelligence about operations (the thing)
   - Matches buyer language: Operations leaders think in terms of "operations"

2. **From "enterprise teams" to "teams"**
   - Enterprise is the client, not the team
   - Broader, doesn't limit to enterprise context

3. **From "front-line workers" to "outcomes they need"**
   - Doesn't assume human workers (could be robots/systems)
   - More professional and outcome-focused
   - Business language that resonates with buyers

4. **From "what needs to happen" to "outcomes they need"**
   - More professional and direct
   - Outcome-focused (business language)
   - Stronger and more assertive

5. **From hyphen to "by"**
   - More natural English flow
   - Doesn't look AI-generated
   - Human-sounding

6. **Removed timeframe**
   - As requested
   - Keeps focus on outcomes, not speed
