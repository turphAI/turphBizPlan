# FINALIZED MESSAGING FRAMEWORK: Operations Intelligence

## Decision
**Business Tag:** Operations Intelligence (plural noun form)

**Rationale:**
- More concrete: Intelligence about operations (the thing)
- Matches buyer language: Operations leaders think in terms of "operations"
- Clearer value proposition: "Intelligence about operations" is clearer
- More direct and actionable

## KEY MESSAGING POINTS

### What You Do
- Embed with enterprise teams
- Understand what they're actually trying to accomplish (underlying tasks)
- Reimagine how agentic solutions can reshape operations
- Validate through rapid prototyping

### What Makes You Different
- Task-first, not process-first (understand tasks, not just digitize processes)
- Fast: 4-5 weeks instead of 6-12 months
- Validated: Working prototypes, not just reports
- Embedded: On-site understanding, not remote consulting

### Who You Serve
- Enterprise operations teams
- Operations leaders (VP Operations, Chief Operating Officers)
- Companies looking to reshape operations, not just optimize existing processes
