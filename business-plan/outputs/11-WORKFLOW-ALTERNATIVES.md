# Alternative Words to "Workflow" for Intelligence Framework

## The Question
You like "Workflow Intelligence" but feel "workflow" might be a bit off. What word sits between "Workflow" (too technical/step-focused?) and "Process" (too abstract/consulting-focused?)?

## Word Spectrum Analysis

**Workflow** ‚Üí [Gap] ‚Üí **Process**

- **Workflow**: Sequential steps, technical, flow-oriented
- **Process**: Abstract procedures, consulting-focused, systematic methods
- **Gap**: Something that's more business-focused, less technical, but still concrete

## Alternative Options (Keeping "Intelligence")

### Option 1: Operations Intelligence
**Why it works:**
- More business-focused than "workflow"
- Less abstract than "process"
- Appeals to operations leaders (your buyers)
- Already evaluated in Framework 2

**Consideration:** You already have this option - is this what you're leaning toward?
