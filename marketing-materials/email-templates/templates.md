# Email Templates

## Initial Outreach Template

**Use this to reach out to people in your network**

### Subject Line (Pick one):
```
Quick question about [Company]'s workflow optimization
```

or

```
Curious about your biggest workflow challenge right now
```

or

```
Thought of you - exploring workflow improvement opportunities
```

### Email Body:

```
Hi [Name],

I'm exploring helping enterprises identify workflow improvements through embedded 
prototyping—basically, I embed with teams for 4-5 weeks, understand their actual 
workflows, rapidly prototype solutions with AI, and validate what works with real users.

Given your work in [specific area/role], I'd love to understand what workflow 
challenges are highest priority for your team right now.

Would you have 20 minutes for a quick call in the next week or two? No pitch, 
just curious about what you're working on.

[Your Name]
[Your Email]
[Your Phone]

P.S. If it helps, here's a quick overview: [Link to one-pager PDF or landing page]
```

## Pitch Email (After Qualified Lead)

```
Subject: Potential engagement for [specific problem]

Hi [Name],

Based on our conversations, I think we could help [Company] quickly validate 
improvements in [specific area].

Here's what I'm thinking:

**The Challenge**:  
[Restate their problem clearly]

**Our Approach** (4-5 weeks):  
1. Embed with your team, deeply understand the current workflow
2. Rapidly prototype 2-3 potential solutions using AI acceleration
3. Validate what works with real users/data
4. Deliver roadmap with clear next steps

**Investment**: $25,000 (fixed fee, includes everything above)

**Outcome**: Your team will know exactly what improvements work, have prototypes 
you can iterate on, and have a clear roadmap to implement.

Interested in exploring this further? I can walk you through the engagement in 
more detail.

[Your Name]
```

## Notes

- **Personalize**: Every email should reference something specific about them/their company
- **Short & scannable**: Keep emails concise
- **Clear CTA**: Always end with a specific ask (call? meeting? feedback?)
- **Track**: Document responses and follow-ups in `/business-plan/research/`
- **Iterate**: Update templates based on response rates
