## 1. MARKET DEFINITION & SIZE

### What is Forward-Deployed AI Workflow Prototyping?

**Definition:** Embedded technical expertise deployed on-site with enterprise teams to discover, prototype, and validate workflow improvements using AI-accelerated tools, grounded in real user research and operational data.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Embedded (on-site, immersed in user environment)
- Design-research driven (user interviews, workflow mapping)
- AI-accelerated (rapid iteration, faster prototyping)
- Validation-focused (real user testing before implementation)
- Time-bound (typically 4-6 week engagements)

**NOT included in this market:**
- General management consulting
- Enterprise software implementation
- Long-term managed services
- Strategic advisory
- Custom software development

## 6. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

### Direct Competitors (Embedded Prototyping Services)

**Who Else Does This?**

Very few pure-play embedded prototyping services exist. Most options are:

1. **Management Consulting Firms** (McKinsey, Bain, Deloitte)
   - **Approach:** Project-based consulting + implementation
   - **Strengths:** Brand, resources, industry relationships
   - **Weaknesses:** Expensive ($500K-$2M+ per project), slow, generic AI knowledge, not specialized in prototyping
   - **Price Range:** $500K-$5M+ per engagement
   - **Duration:** 6-12+ months
   - **Your Advantage:** Faster, cheaper, specialized in rapid prototyping, AI-accelerated

2. **AI/ML Services Firms** (Scale AI, Anduril, others)
   - **Approach:** AI platform + implementation services
   - **Strengths:** Deep tech expertise, fast execution
   - **Weaknesses:** Tech-first (not user-centered), limited business consulting, domain knowledge varies
   - **Price Range:** $250K-$1M per engagement
   - **Duration:** 8-16 weeks
   - **Your Advantage:** User-research driven, design-thinking approach, business outcomes focused

3. **Design & UX Consulting** (Frog, IDEO, etc.)
   - **Approach:** Design research + prototyping
   - **Strengths:** User research expertise, design thinking, rapid prototyping
   - **Weaknesses:** Limited AI expertise, smaller team capacity, less business-focused
   - **Price Range:** $150K-$500K per engagement
   - **Duration:** 6-12 weeks
   - **Your Advantage:** AI-accelerated speed, business outcomes focus, workflow optimization not just UX

4. **In-House Teams**
   - **Approach:** Build internal AI prototyping capability
   - **Strengths:** Long-term control, knowledge stays internal
   - **Weaknesses:** Expensive ($200-300K+ per engineer/year), slow to ramp (18-24 months), talent shortage, need multiple specialists
   - **Cost:** $1-3M+ per year (3-5 engineers)
   - **Your Advantage:** 4-5 week engagement vs. 18-24 month hiring, lower fixed cost, proven results
