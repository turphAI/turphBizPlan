# Networking Tracking & Research

Use this document to track your networking conversations and gather feedback.

## Networking Contacts

Create a list of 20-30 people you want to reach out to:

| Name | Company | Role | Market | Relationship | Contact | Status | Notes |
|------|---------|------|--------|--------------|---------|--------|-------|
| | | | Finance/Construction/Defense/Ecommerce | | Email/Phone/LinkedIn | Not contacted/Scheduled/Completed | |

**How to use**:
- Add all contacts first
- Update Status as you reach out
- Add Notes after each conversation
- Use this to identify patterns and best opportunities

## Template: Conversation Log Entry

Use this template for each conversation:

```markdown
### Conversation [#]: [Name] - [Company]
**Date**: [Date]  
**Duration**: [30 min / 45 min / 60 min]  
**Market**: [Financial Services / Construction / Defense / E-commerce]

**Pain Points**:
1. 
2. 
3. 

**Budget**: 
- Mentioned range? [Yes/No]
- Amount: [If yes]
- Comfort level with consulting spend: [High/Medium/Low]

**Timeline**:
- When could they start? [ASAP / 1-2 months / 3+ months]
- Urgency: [Urgent / Soon / Exploring / No timeline]

**Decision Making**:
- Who makes final decision? [Title]
- Other stakeholders? [Roles]
- Approval complexity? [Simple / Moderate / Complex]

**Interest**: [High / Medium / Low]

**Next Steps**:
- [ ] Send follow-up email
- [ ] Schedule deeper conversation
- [ ] Request intro to [person]
- [ ] Propose engagement

**Notes**:
[Any other observations, personality notes, specific context]

**When to follow up**: [Date]
```
