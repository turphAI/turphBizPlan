## Phase 1: Market Research & Opportunity Analysis

### Objectives

1. **TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis**
   - Define total addressable market (global AI services)
   - Calculate serviceable addressable market (your 4 target segments)
   - Determine serviceable obtainable market (realistic 5-year capture)

2. **Market Trends**
   - Document 5-6 macro trends driving forward-deployed AI adoption
   - Connect each to your business opportunity
   - Provide growth rates and timelines

3. **Customer Personas & Buyer Journey**
   - Create 3-5 detailed personas per market
   - Map their pain points and decision process
   - Identify decision-makers and influencers

4. **Segment Deep Dive**
   - Financial Services: Advisor workflows, compliance, customer journey
   - Defense/Aerospace: Project workflows, R&D optimization, team coordination
   - Construction/Environmental: Job site coordination, compliance, project management
   - E-commerce: Conversion optimization, customer journey, operations

5. **Go-to-Market Positioning**
   - Which market to target first for early traction
   - Why each market matters for your business
   - Sequential entry strategy

### Deliverables

- `business-plan/outputs/02-market-analysis.md` (6-8 pages)
- Market sizing spreadsheet with calculations
- Persona profiles (1 page each)
- Segment analysis summary
- TAM/SAM/SOM visualization
