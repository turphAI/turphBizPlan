# PROFESSIONAL ALTERNATIVES: Replacing "What Needs to Happen"

## The Challenge
Option 3B-B works well, but "what needs to happen" feels too soft and non-professional. Need more direct, professional alternatives that maintain the flow.

## Current (Option 3B-B):
"Principal at Operations Intelligence | I embed with teams to understand what needs to happen, then reimagine how agentic solutions can accomplish it differently by prototyping and validating what actually works."

**Problem:** "What needs to happen" feels too soft and non-professional.

---

## OPTION 1: "What They're Trying to Accomplish"

"Principal at Operations Intelligence | I embed with teams to understand what they're trying to accomplish, then reimagine how agentic solutions can accomplish it differently by prototyping and validating what actually works."

**Why it works:**
- More direct and professional
- "Trying to accomplish" - action-oriented
- Clear focus on outcomes
- Professional tone

---

## OPTION 2: "What They Need to Accomplish"

"Principal at Operations Intelligence | I embed with teams to understand what they need to accomplish, then reimagine how agentic solutions can accomplish it differently by prototyping and validating what actually works."

- Emphasizes necessity/requirement
- More assertive tone
