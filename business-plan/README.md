# Business Plan Development

This folder contains everything related to developing your comprehensive business plan.

## Structure

### `/data-collection/`
- `DATA_COLLECTION_TEMPLATE.md` - Main data collection form
  - Fill this in as you develop your strategy
  - Reference for all business plan sections

### `/outputs/`
Generated business plan sections (created as you progress):
- `01-executive-summary.md`
- `02-market-analysis.md`
- `03-competitive-analysis.md`
- `04-offering.md`
- `05-go-to-market.md`
- `06-financials.md`
- `07-team.md`
- `BUSINESS_PLAN.md` (complete integrated plan)

### `/research/`
Supporting research and reference materials:
- Market sizing data
- Competitor research
- Industry reports
- Customer insights from networking

## Workflow

1. **Data Collection** - Fill in `DATA_COLLECTION_TEMPLATE.md` as you learn
2. **Market Research** - Gather data for `/research/`
3. **Skill-Based Generation** - Use Agent Skills to generate sections in `/outputs/`
4. **Synthesis** - Create final `BUSINESS_PLAN.md` from all sections
